sSunPLS Vault Guide

What is sSunPLS?

sSunPLS is an auto-compounding vault for the SunPLS/WPLS liquidity pool on PulseChain. When you provide liquidity to that pool, you earn trading fees. Normally you'd have to manually claim and reinvest those fees — this vault does it for you, automatically, on every harvest.

Your sSunPLS shares grow in LP value over time. You never need to click "claim" — just stake and withdraw when you're ready.

How to Stake

1
Get LP tokens first. Add liquidity to the SunPLS/WPLS pair on PulseX to receive LP tokens in your wallet.
2
Connect your wallet (top right). It will switch to PulseChain automatically.
3
Enter your LP amount in the Stake section and click Stake LP Tokens. The first deposit requires an approval transaction.
4
Receive sSunPLS shares. These represent your proportional stake in the vault. Hold them — they appreciate automatically as fees are compounded in.

How to Withdraw

1
To exit partially, enter a share amount and click Withdraw Partial.
2
To exit fully, click Withdraw All. You receive your original LP plus all compounded gains.

What is Auto-Compounding?

Every time someone calls Harvest, the vault collects the accumulated trading fees from the pool, converts them into more LP tokens, and adds them back to the vault. This increases the LP value of every sSunPLS share — so your position grows without any action on your part.

Harvest is permissionless — anyone can trigger it. There is a 1-hour cooldown between harvests.

Exchange Rate

The exchange rate shown at the top is how many LP tokens each sSunPLS share is worth. It starts at 1.0 and increases over time as fees compound in. A rate of 1.05 means your shares are worth 5% more LP than when you deposited.

Immutable & Ownerless

The vault contract has no admin keys and cannot be upgraded. It operates autonomously on PulseChain.
