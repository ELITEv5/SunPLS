# SunPLS Disclaimer

SunPLS is an experimental decentralized financial protocol deployed on PulseChain.

This repository contains smart contracts, documentation, and research materials related to the SunPLS protocol.

By interacting with the SunPLS system, users acknowledge and accept the risks described in this document.

---

# Experimental Software

SunPLS is experimental software.

The protocol is part of an ongoing exploration into **autonomous monetary systems** and feedback-based stabilization mechanisms inspired by the ProjectUSD research specifications.

Although extensive care may have been taken in the design and implementation of the system, there is **no guarantee that the protocol will behave as expected under all market conditions**.

Users should treat SunPLS as experimental financial infrastructure.

---

The SunPLS protocol provides **no guarantees** regarding:

All smart contracts carry inherent risks.

Potential risks include but are not limited to:

# Market Risk

SunPLS depends on the value of its collateral asset (PLS).

If the price of PLS experiences significant volatility or rapid declines, the system may experience:

- large liquidation events
- temporary peg deviations
- market instability

These outcomes are inherent to collateralized financial systems.

---

# Oracle Risk

The protocol relies on price data from on-chain liquidity pools.

Although safeguards such as TWAP calculations and fallback mechanisms exist, oracle systems may still experience issues including:

- price manipulation attempts
- liquidity distortions
- stale price data
- temporary outages

These conditions may affect protocol behavior.
