📖 How Liquidations & Redemptions Work ▼

  • Liquidation Trigger: Vaults below 110% health ratio can be liquidated
  • Minimum Repay: 20% of total debt (automatically calculated)
  • Dutch Auction Bonus: Starts at 2% and increases to 5% over 3 hours from when the vault first went underwater
  • Liquidation Cooldown: 10 minutes between liquidations of the same vault
  • Process: Approve SunPLS → liquidate vault → receive PLS reward
  • Interest Buffer: Dashboard adds 1% buffer to handle debt growth during the transaction, capped at total vault debt
  • 🔄 Redemptions live here: Use the Redeemable tab below to find eligible vaults and redeem SunPLS for PLS — no need to copy-paste addresses manually
  • Redemption Eligibility (v1.4): Only vaults at or below 130% CR are eligible — vaults above 130% are immune
  • Redemption Rate: R = controller's redemption value in WPLS per SunPLS — burn N SunPLS, receive N × R / 1e18 PLS
  • Redemption Fee: 0.5% fee stays with the vault owner as compensation
  • Anti-Griefing Gap: Vault cannot be liquidated within 5 minutes of being redeemed against
  • Auto-Rescan: Dashboard refreshes every 2 minutes
